Legal Document Bot

AI-powered legal consultation and document generation platform

Project Overview

Professional freelance project developed via Upwork - a comprehensive AI-powered legal assistance Telegram bot providing 24/7 legal consultations, automated document generation, and subscription-based services for Georgian legal technology automation.

Professional Freelance Project
12+ Types Legal Documents
24/7 AI Assistance

Business Challenge & Solution

Market Need

Small businesses and individuals in Georgia needed affordable, accessible legal assistance and document generation services without the cost of traditional legal consultations.

Technical Solution

Built a comprehensive Telegram bot platform combining GPT-4 AI consultation, automated document generation, subscription management, and payment processing for scalable legal services.

Core AI Features

Smart Legal Consultation

GPT-4 powered AI providing context-aware responses to legal questions with multi-step clarification workflows

Document Generation

Automated creation of 12+ legal document types including contracts, claims, powers of attorney, and complaints

Document Analysis

Upload and analyze existing legal documents in multiple formats (.docx, .pdf, .txt) with AI-powered review

Subscription Services

Tiered pricing with YooKassa payment integration, usage tracking, and automated billing management

Technical Implementation

AI & NLP Integration

  • OpenAI API integration with GPT-4, O3-mini, and O4-mini models
  • Specialized legal prompts and templates for domain-specific responses
  • Multi-step dialogue system for complex legal question analysis
  • Document processing with python-docx and pypdf libraries

Business Logic & Architecture

  • Modern Python 3.13+ with async/await patterns for scalability
  • MongoDB for user data, session management, and subscription tracking
  • YooKassa API integration for payment processing and billing
  • APScheduler for automated subscription checks and notifications

Platform Architecture

User Experience Layer

Interactive Telegram interface with button-based navigation, file upload support, conversation history, and multi-language support (Russian/English).

Business Management

Subscription tiers, automated billing cycles, payment monitoring with real-time status verification, and usage limiting for non-subscribers.

Document Processing

Specialized templates for different legal document types with JSON-structured generation and professional formatting capabilities.

Professional Impact

Freelance Success

Successfully completed professional project via Upwork, demonstrating ability to deliver enterprise-level legal technology solutions for real clients with commercial requirements.

Technical Achievement

Built production-ready platform combining AI, payment processing, subscription management, and document generation into a scalable legal assistance service.

Business Value

Created accessible legal services platform for Georgian market, enabling affordable legal assistance for small businesses and individuals through AI automation.
