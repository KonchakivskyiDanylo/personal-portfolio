My Journey

Mathematics has been my companion since childhood. During my school years, I participated in mathematical olympiads, which taught me analytical thinking and problem-solving from multiple angles. When choosing my university path, Applied Mathematics at NaUKMA felt like the natural choice - it was here that I discovered programming and how abstract mathematical concepts find practical applications.

Today, I maintain a 93.3 GPA while working as a Research Intern at the Kyiv School of Economics, focusing on Formal Concept Analysis implementations. This combination of theoretical understanding and practical programming drives my current work in AI and machine learning.
